VARIETIES

Ladies' finger

Ladies' finger, also known as okra, is a nutritious vegetable rich in vitamins and fiber, prized for its tender texture and mild flavor. It's a versatile ingredient, perfect for a variety of dishes, from stir-fries to stews.

Tomato

Juicy and tangy, tomatoes are rich in vitamins and antioxidants, ideal for salads, sauces, and soups.

Broccoli

Packed with vitamins and fiber, broccoli offers a crisp texture and a slightly bitter flavor, great for steaming or stir-frying

Carrot

Sweet and crunchy, carrots are high in beta-carotene and perfect for snacking, salads, or cooking.

Potato

Versatile and hearty, potatoes are a staple food, offering comfort in dishes like mashed potatoes, fries, and stews.

Cauliflower

Mild and versatile, cauliflower can be used in a variety of dishes, from low-carb rice alternatives to creamy soups.

Cabage

Crisp and slightly peppery, cabbage is rich in nutrients and great for salads, stir-fries, or as a cooked side dish.

Brinjal

With a meaty texture and subtle flavor, brinjal is excellent for grilling, roasting, or adding depth to curries.

Pumpkin

Sweet and nutritious, pumpkin is perfect for soups, pies, and roasted dishes, packed with vitamins and fiber.
